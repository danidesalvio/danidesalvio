What I Do

The past 2 years I have built custom websites using HTML, CSS, & Javascript at a small real estate tech company. All of the websites that I build are 100% responsive sizing from desktop to mobile devices. I work with each client individually and independently in order to acheive the desired look and feel for their websites. A lot of the problem solving that I do, benefits our parent company's design team in Washington, making faster processes for them and also coming up with solutions to problems or elements that clients desire on their sites.
