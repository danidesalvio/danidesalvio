What I Do

The past 2 years I have built custom websites using HTML, CSS, & Javascript at a small tech company. All sites that I build are 100% responsive sizing from desktop to mobile devices. Not only do I build websites, I also design mockups for sites using Photoshop or Figma and create new site elements for our sitebuilder. I communicate with some of our development team in Costa Rica to finalize new templated block code and also work a lot with our parent company's design team in Washington to help them with building or fixing sites. I am lucky to also have a background in video production, because I am given the freedom to edit videos for clients and experiment with After Effects for internal projects.
